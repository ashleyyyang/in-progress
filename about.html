Hi — I’m Ashley, a UX designer :-)

I am currently designing analytical marketing dashboards at Postmedia I/O.

Originally from the USA, I moved to Canada to study Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo. When I’m not in class, you can find me drawing, coding (this website!), and working in a solar car team.

I design to help people understand information that matters to them, whether I’m working on:
- a website to help people start learning another language
- an analytical dashboard to guide marketing decisions, or
- solar car dashboard to inform drivers safely and efficiently.
